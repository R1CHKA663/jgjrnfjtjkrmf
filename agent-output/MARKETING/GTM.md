## 7) Offer strategy (MVP-safe)
**Welcome offer (simple and low abuse):**
- Option A: **Deposit match up to $50** (wagering requirement 30x, min odds rules)
- Option B (if no deposits yet): **Daily free roll** (tiny EV, requires login)

**Retention offers**
- Daily missions: 3 tasks (e.g., place 10 bets, play 3 games, wager $X)
- Weekly cashback: % of net loss, tiered by VIP

**Anti-abuse basics**
- Cap bonuses per device/IP patterns
- Require email verification before withdrawals
- Track bonus-to-cash conversion and suspicious cashouts
